Kristelle Marianna The Bride

That was such a special experience and I can't wait to be sealed to Porter and start our eternal family. I have been in choirs my whole life and love all things music. I'm currently at UVU studying Exercise Science with dreams of becoming a Physical Therapist. I can't wait for what life will bring for Porter and I!
